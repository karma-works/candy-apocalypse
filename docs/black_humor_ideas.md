## 🎯 Summary: The Comedy Formula

**Every element should answer: "Would this make the player laugh?"**

1. **Death = Comedy** - No frustration, just laughs
2. **Physics = Toys** - Ragdolls, bounces, delays
3. **Enemies = Characters** - Each has personality
4. **Sound = Voice** - Literal "pew pew" sounds
5. **UI = Satire** - Health bar says "Will to Live"
6. **Achievements = Memes** - "NICE." at 69 kills
7. **Easter Eggs = Tributes** - Moorhuhn, Angry Birds, classic Doom
8. **Loading = Comedy** - Tips are jokes
9. **Quota = Voice Lines** - Enemies have personality
10. **Social = Shareable** - Funny moments auto-captured

---

**Remember**: If it doesn't make the player go "Haha, nice!", it doesn't belong in the game.
